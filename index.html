FAQ

Clear answers to reduce planning friction.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends and large installs book first. If you’re within a short window, submit your details and we’ll confirm availability quickly.

Can you install outdoors?

Yes, with constraints. Heat, wind, sun exposure, and surface conditions affect performance; we’ll recommend the safest setup for your location.

How long do balloons last?

Indoors typically lasts longer than outdoors. We’ll advise on timing so it looks best during your event window.

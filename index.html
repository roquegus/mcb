Common questions

Quick answers to help you plan with confidence.

How far in advance should I book?

For weekends and larger installs, earlier is better. If you have a short timeframe, submit a request and we’ll confirm availability.

Can you install outdoors?

Yes, with guidance and limitations based on heat, wind, sun exposure, and surface conditions. We’ll recommend the safest setup for your site.

How long do balloons last?

Indoors often lasts longer than outdoors. We’ll advise based on your environment and timing so it looks best during your event.
